Pop-ups

Pop-ups simplify site functions by appearing as an overlay over existing page content. They may exist as one or multiple steps. They adopt the same rules as normal page content but feature a header and a close button. The background page 'dims' while dialogs are active. A black layer #000000 at 80% is used to create this effect.

Buttons must be right-aligned as shown in the screenshot, and usually consist of [Cancel] and [Save] (in that order).



Pop-up Forms

There are two ways to display forms inside a pop-up window, which are displayed below. For more information on form styles, please refer to the forms page.

Top-aligned Form Labels

Use top-aligned form labels when:

  • Form labels are really long (i.e. localization).

Do not use top-aligned form labels when:

  • Forms are really long. Use right-aligned form labels to help reduce vertical screen space. See below for more information.
